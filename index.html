About Samvidhan Playbook

Samvidhan Playbook is an interactive educational game designed to teach the Indian Constitution through engaging scenarios and real-life situations. Learn about fundamental rights, duties, and constitutional principles while having fun!

Each question presents a scenario where constitutional knowledge is applied, helping you understand how the Constitution protects citizens in everyday situations.
